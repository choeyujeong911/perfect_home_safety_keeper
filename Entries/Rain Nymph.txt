DESCRIPTION:
Rain Nymphs are a subspecies of Nymph that are most active during rain storms.
They are faint humanoids with a glow in their eyes that often appear near windows and doors, asking to be let inside.
Homeowners afficted with Rain Nymphs often report flooding.
[설명]


DANGER:
The floods Rain Nymphs cause can lead to water damage, which can be both costly and dangerous to a home.
[피해]


SOLUTION:
Rain Nymphs are very preventable, in that their pleas to be let inside can simply be ignored.
It is advised to stay away from doors and windows during rain storms, to avoid having to endure a Rain Nymph's various temptations and threats.
[대응]
