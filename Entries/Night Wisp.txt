DESCRIPTION:
Night Wisps are a subspecies of wisp in the form of bright, floating lights.
They are known to hover outside windows at night while whispering promises of wealth and good fortune.
[설명]


DANGER:
Night Wisps are not dangerous to humans unless listened to.
Refrain from listening to their whispers and following their directions.
[피해]


SOLUTION:
Night Wisps will move on to other homes after a week or so if they are ignored consistently.
It is advised to drown out their whispers with music or wearing sleeping masks to prevent sleep disruption.
[대응]
