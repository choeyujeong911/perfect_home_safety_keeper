DESCRIPTION:
Dorcha is the one who delivers.
Homeowners experiencing Dorcha commonly report feelings of immense dread, and the sensation of being followed or pursued.
They may also experience visions of a great lurking shadow.
[설명]


DANGER:
Dorcha will deliver that which it seeks.
There is no coming back from deliverance.
Dorcha is otherwise harmless to humans it does not seek.
[피해]


SOLUTION:
To be sought by Dorcha is to be found by Dorcha.
If you or a loved one are sought by Dorcha, it is advised to grieve, and find peace through acceptance.
[대응]
