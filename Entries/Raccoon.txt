Raccoons are small nocturnal mammals with grey and brown fur known for their tendency to rummage through garbage cans or nest in attics.
Homeowners infested with Raccoons often report frequent garbage spills, or clattering sounds at night.

Raccoons are capable of biting humans and spreading deadly diseases such as rabies, so caution around them is advised.
