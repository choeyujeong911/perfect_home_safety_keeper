DESCRIPTION:
House Flies are small flying creatures that are known to commonly reside inside homes and frequently surround those whose death is impending.
Homeowners afflicted with House Flies commonly report buzzing sounds and thoughts of decay.
[설명]
집파리는 실내 혹은 사체 주변에 주로 머무는 작은 날벌레입니다.
집파리로 고통받는 가정에서는 주로 윙윙거리는 소리와 부패에 대한 의심이 보고됩니다.

DANGER:
House Flies are no immediate danger to humans, but are known disease carriers.
They frequently inhabit kitchens and other areas used to prepare food, increasing the risk of disease.
